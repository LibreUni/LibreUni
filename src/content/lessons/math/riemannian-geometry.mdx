# Riemannian Geometry

Riemannian geometry is the branch of differential geometry that studies Riemannian manifolds—manifolds equipped with a metric tensor.

## The Metric Tensor

A **Riemannian metric** $g$ is a collection of inner products on the tangent spaces of a manifold. In coordinates, it is represented by a symmetric matrix $g_{ij}$.

## Geodesics

**Geodesics** are the "straightest possible" paths on a manifold. They generalize the notion of a line to curved spaces. In GR, particles move along geodesics in spacetime.

## Curvature Tensors

Curvature in Riemannian geometry is captured by the **Riemann Curvature Tensor** $R^l_{ijk}$. It measures the extent to which parallel transport depends on the path taken.
